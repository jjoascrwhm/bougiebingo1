Empowering Future Leaders

The El Paso–Southern New Mexico Chapter of Jack and Jill of America, Inc. represents a distinguished legacy of nurturing exceptional African American children through comprehensive programming in education, culture, civic engagement, and recreation.

Our chapter creates transformative experiences that develop confident, well-rounded young leaders prepared to excel in an ever-changing world. Through events like Bougie Bingo, we strengthen our community while celebrating the rich cultural heritage and bright futures of our families.

Community Partner

Event Beneficiary

Boys & Girls Club of El Paso

For over six decades, the Boys & Girls Club of El Paso has served as a beacon of hope and opportunity for thousands of young people throughout our community. Their mission to enable all young people to reach their full potential as productive, caring, and responsible citizens aligns perfectly with our values.

Proceeds from Bougie Bingo will directly support the Club's after-school programs, academic enrichment initiatives, character development activities, and healthy lifestyle programming. Together, we're investing in the next generation of leaders and ensuring every child has access to the resources they need to thrive.
