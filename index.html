Philanthropic Partners

Supporting youth locally and nationally

Proceeds from Bougie Bingo benefit both local and national partners committed to empowering children and strengthening communities.

National Impact
Jack and Jill of America Foundation

The Jack and Jill of America Foundation is the philanthropic arm of Jack and Jill of America, Inc., investing in programs that create a more just and equitable future for Black children and families.

Through scholarships, grants, and strategic partnerships, the Foundation advances education, leadership, and community development initiatives nationwide.

Scholarships Education & Literacy Health & Wellness Civic Engagement
Local Impact
Boys & Girls Club of El Paso

The Boys & Girls Club of El Paso provides safe, supportive spaces where young people can learn, grow, and reach their full potential. From academic support to mentorship and enrichment activities, the Club is a vital resource for local youth.
