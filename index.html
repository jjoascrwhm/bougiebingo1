Our Chapter

About EPSNM Chapter

The El Paso‚ÄìSouthern New Mexico Chapter of Jack and Jill of America, Inc. is a proud member of the nation's premier African American family organization, dedicated to nurturing future leaders by strengthening children through leadership development, volunteer service, philanthropic giving, and civic duty.

Our chapter serves families across the El Paso and Southern New Mexico region, creating cultural, educational, and social opportunities that enrich the lives of children ages 2-19. Through events like Bougie Bingo, we raise funds to support local and national initiatives that make a lasting impact on our communities.

Founded on the principles of motherhood and service, our members are committed to developing the leadership skills and cultural awareness of our children while giving back to the communities we call home.
